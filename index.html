Welcome! You are in the information portal of one of the famous games Lineage 2.

Information on this portal actually till Interlude Chronicles.

This is a rework of one of the information portals l2p.lacrafter.ru

Due to country restrictions on l2p.lacrafter.ru because it has a Russian domain name it couldn't be displayed in many countries.

Here you will find out how physics in Lineage 2 actually works.

This site absolutely does not have any advertisements, feel free to share it with your friends.
