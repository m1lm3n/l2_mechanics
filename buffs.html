FAQ

Read this. As in, don't ignore it. Or you can ignore it and ask stupid questions I already answered and embarrass yourself. Up to you.

Q. How come my stat didn't increase by as much as you said it would?

A. This is usually, in the case of P.Atk., P.Def., M.Atk., and M.Def., directly because of your Masteries. Masteries are not boosted by buffs on these stats which in the end means you get less than the buff said it would. If you really want it exact, you have no choice right now but to do the math yourself by looking at the Status Window section of this website.

For Critical, the buffs multiply on your base Critical individually and then add together in the end. It may sound complicated but it's not really too bad. Again for more info on that, look at the Status Window section.

Lastly, the game does round numbers down. What your status window shows is only a rounded number. The game actually uses the full decimal value as far as I know.

If, however, you think the buff really is wrong, please send me a message. We'll take a look at what's wrong with it. Everyone makes mistakes, and unlike some places I'll openly admit I do it a lot.

Q. How much does Holy Weapon, Dance of Light or Holy Blade boost damage on monsters? Do any of them stack?

None of them stack. The damage boost varies depending on which monster you are fighting. First of all the monster must be weak to Holy attribute, which is seen by "Weak to Sacred Attack" in the monster's description. Most monsters with a Holy weakness take 20% more damage from Holy attacks. The exceptions are the Vampires from Forest of the Dead and the Demon type monsters (but not Undead) from Imperial Tomb, Forge of the Gods and the Rift, which only take 10% more damage.

Q. It says the "blah blah HP or MP per tick" is this, but in game it's totally different. Why!

The numbers you'll see here are just base values. I do not know the formula for per tick stuff at present. The only exception should be the % changes. Percent changes should be correct as listed but it's still confusing because of the regen formulas.
